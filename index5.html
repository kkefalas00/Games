Thundercats is the first animation created in collaboration with child psychologists. This happened because the creators of the series, wanted to pass on to children in a prudent way for their age, situations such as death and life. That's why the Thundercats do not die. Each episode is a life lesson, based on the Thundera code, not only for Lion-O and the rest of the thundercats but also for parents and children watching the series. Just as Thundercats follow the four basic principles of Thundera (justice, truth, honor, loyalty), so children and their parents should follow these fundamental principles.
